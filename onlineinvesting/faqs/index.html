Online Investing App FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Online Investing App?

Westpac Online Investing's tailor made share trading platform for your smartphone and iPad™.

What smartphones do you support?

We support any smartphone with Apple™ iOS 4.3+ or Android™ 2.3+.

What tablets do you support?

We currently support any iPad™ with Apple™ iOS 4.3+.

How do I access the Online Investing App?

Search for Westpac Online Investing in the App Store™ or Google Play™ store and install.

Or...

Upgrade the Westpac Mobile Banking App via the Apple App Store™ or Google Play™ store (Android). Then click on the 'Online Investing' button to download the Online Investing App.

When does the new App launch?

The Online Investing App will be available from early July 2012.

What Username and Password do I use?

Use your existing Online Investing Username and Password to log in.

I have an iPhone™ / Android™ device and cannot access Online Investing through the Westpac Mobile Banking app?

Ensure you have the latest Westpac Mobile Banking App. Further, your device needs to be running the Apple™ iOS 4.3+ or Android™ 2.3+ operating system. To update to the latest Westpac Banking App visit the Apple App Store™ or Google Play™. To update your device's operating system, please consult your device manual or supplier.

What if I need help?
Please email us or alternatively contact our help desk on 13 13 31 between 7am and 7pm, Monday-Friday.

What if I use a Blackberry™, Windows Mobile™ or other unsupported device?

You can still use the full site at onlineinvesting.westpac.com.au

Can I view my cash balance from the Online Investing App?

Yes, you will be able to view the cash balances of Westpac integrated cash accounts.

Can I use my BT margin loan to settle trades from the Online Investing App?

Yes, simply select your BT margin lending account within the orders page.

Can I trade options on the Online Investing App?

No, you cannot currently trade options through the Online Investing App. However, you can get option quotes via the quotes section.

What happens to the m.onlineinvesting.westpac.com.au website?

This site will remain active for a short period after the new Online Investing App is released. After this period you will be redirected to the App Store or Google Play store to download the new App.

Do I need to pay to download or use the Online Investing App?

The App is free to download, however you do need to be a Westpac Online Investing customer to use it. Please note standard brokerage rates, fees and charges still apply.
