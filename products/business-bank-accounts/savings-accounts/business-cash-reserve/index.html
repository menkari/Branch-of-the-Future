  • What you'll need to apply

    To apply:

    • You must have a business registered in Australia, operating as either:
      • A sole trader, individual or partnership
      • A public or proprietary company
      • An association or trust.
    • You don’t need to be an existing Westpac customer to apply
    • You will  need to provide trustee and beneficiary details if you're applying for a trust account
    • You don't need to provide your Australian Business Number (ABN) or tax file number (TFN) .This is not compulsory and we can open your account without these details.  When your account is operating, however, tax may be withheld if we do not have your ABN or TFN
    • We will need to be able to identify you and every signatory to the account. Westpac customers, just need to provide their Westpac 8-digit customer ID and Australian driver's licence details (where applicable). New customers  will  need to go to a branch to be identified with the account documents we'll mail to them. Details of our ID requirements are on our public site.
