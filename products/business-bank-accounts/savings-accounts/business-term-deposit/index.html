Business Term Deposit

Products & Solutions Business bank accounts

Earn up to 2.50% p.a. for a 6 month term deposit

  • No set-up, monthly service or management fees
  • Interest paid monthly, annually or at maturity (depending on term)
  • Minimum opening balance $5,000

  • Features

    Flexible interest payment options

    • Paid at maturity - for terms of 12 months or less
    • Paid monthly - for terms of 1 to 60 months (inclusive)
    • Paid annually - for terms of greater than 12 months (for terms not equalling an exact year, the remaining interest is paid at the end of the term).

    Maturity options

    Once your term deposit matures you can:

    • Reinvest all, part or more than your principal and interest earned for a new term of your choice
    • Withdraw funds (by cheque, cash or transfer to your business account).

    Rates

    • Rates for new or renewal term deposits are subject to change at any time.
    • Once a term deposit account is opened the rate applicable to the account will not change (unless the deposit or part of it is repaid early).

  • What you'll need to apply

    To open a business term deposit online you must:

    • Have an eligible business registered in Australia
    • Provide trustee and beneficiary details if you're applying for a trust account
    • (and every signatory an account) must be identified according to Westpac's ID standard

    Lock it in - Send us quick online enquiry to secure your new business term deposit.
