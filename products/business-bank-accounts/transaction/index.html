    1. Westpac Foreign Transaction Fee
      This fee will be incorporated into the $AUD transaction amount shown on your statement:

      (a) 3% of the $AUD transaction amount where a transaction is made in foreign currency and the merchant or financial institution accepting the card is located outside Australia ( 2% Westpac Processing Fee plus 1% Westpac On-Charged Scheme Fee)

      (b) 2.2% of the $AUD transaction amount where a transaction is made in foreign currency and the merchant or financial institution accepting the card is located in Australia (2% Westpac Processing Fee plus  0.2% Westpac On-Charged Scheme Fee).
