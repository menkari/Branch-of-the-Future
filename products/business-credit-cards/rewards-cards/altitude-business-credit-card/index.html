Products & Solutions Business credit cards

Altitude Business credit cards - A business credit card that allows you to earn Altitude points on your business spend.

  • 2 cards linked to 1 account (Altitude Business American Express® Card and Altitude Business MasterCard®)
  • Up to 45 days interest free on purchases1
  • Business credit limit from $1,000 up to $30,000.

  • Features
    • Earn 2 Altitude points per $1 spent on purchases using your Altitude Business American Express® Card2
    • Earn 1 Altitude point per $1 spent on purchases using your Altitude Business MasterCard2.
    • The account holder is liable for all transactions.

  • Fees

    Annual fee: $100

    For details on other fees and charges please visit full website. Fees and charges (including annual fee) may change.

  • Rates
    TransactionVariable interest rates
    Purchases19.99% p.a.
    Cash advances21.59% p.a.

  • What you'll need to apply

    What information do I need?

    • Your monthly personal after-tax income
    • Your current employment details (address and phone number)
    • Your Australian driver's licence number (if you have one)
    • Details of your current financial commitments
    • ABN or ACN of your company
    • Your accountant's name and phone number
    • Business income and expenses from your last completed tax return.

    Who can apply?
    To be eligible to apply, please confirm that:

    1. You intend to use these cards wholly or predominantly for business or investment purposes (other than investment in residential property)
    2. Your business has been trading for the last full financial year
    3. You’re a permanent Australian resident, aged 18 years or over.
