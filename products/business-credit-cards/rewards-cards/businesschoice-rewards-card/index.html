BusinessChoice Rewards credit card - Earn 1 Qantas Frequent Flyer point for every $1.50 spent on purchases1.

  • Available as a BusinessChoice Rewards Visa or BusinessChoice Rewards MasterCard®
  • Up to 45 days interest free on purchases2
  • Business credit limit from $2,000 up to $50,000.

  • Features
    • Up to 10 cards for each account with individual credit limits from $1,000.
    • Enjoy a range of complimentary insurance covers as set out in the policy including Interstate Flight Inconvenience cover3
    • Extra security with Cardshield® to guard against fraud4
    • Cardholders earn Qantas Frequent Flyer points to redeem for flights or other rewards. They must be a member of the Qantas Frequent Flyer program and advise us of their valid membership number. To learn more visit the full website.
    • Business and Principals applying are jointly and severely liable for all transactions.

  • Fees

    Annual card fee: $150 for each card.

    For details on other fees and charges please visit full website. Fees and charges (including annual fee) may change.

  • Rates
    TransactionVariable interest rates
    Purchases18.99% p.a.
    Cash advances18.99% p.a.

  • What you'll need to apply

    You don't need to be an existing Westpac customer but each individual principal must:

    • Be a permanent Australian resident (or New Zealand citizen who reside in Australia)
    • Be 18 years or over
    • Not be bankrupt, have any defaults or judgements listed with any credit reporting agency.

    Your business (sole trader, partnership, company or trust) must be registered in Australia and must have been trading for the last full financial year.

    You'll need:

    • Your personal details - residential address, phone number and driver's licence number (if you have one)
    • Your business details - including ABN/ACN, trustee's details (if applicable)
    • Your personal and business finances including after-tax income
    • Accountant or financial controller details.

    You will need to confirm these cards are to be used wholly or predominantly for business or investment purposes (other than investment in residential property).
