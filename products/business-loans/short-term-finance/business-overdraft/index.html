Business Overdraft

Products & Solutions Business loans

A flexible solution to paying wages and other business expenses.

  • Access your overdraft through your business transaction account, up to an agreed limit
  • Needs to be secured by residential, commercial or rural property
  • Flexible repayment options.

  • Features
    • Access cash to tender for large jobs
    • Negotiate better rates with suppliers by paying upfront.

  • Fees

    Every business is different so please contact us to discuss what product is best for your business and the applicable fees.

    Establishment and loan service fees are based on how much you borrow and the value of the property you use to secure the loan.

  • Rates
    • Variable rate, currently 8.53% p.a.
    • Rate is current as at 15 October 2012
    • Interest is calculated daily and charged monthly only on the amount used.

  • What you'll need to apply
    • Your business needs to be registered in Australia
    • Proof of income, typically 2 years profit and loss plus tax returns
    • Secure the overdraft with residential, commercial or rural property
    • Additional documents may be required.
