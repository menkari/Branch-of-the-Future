Business Loan

Products & Solutions Business loans

Starting from $20,000, the business loan is available at fixed or variable interest rates.

  • Loan terms up to 25 years: secured residential property
  • Loan term up to 15 years: secured by commercial or rural property
  • Redraw available on variable rate loan
  • Flexible repayment options on variable rate loan. Choose Interest Only to minimise repayments or Principal and Interest to pay your loan off faster
  • Fixed rate also available.

  • Features
    • Secure the location of your business by financing the purchase of your premises
    • Grow and expand your business with cash to finance long term investment opportunities.

  • Fees

    Every business is different so please contact us to discuss what product is best for your business and the applicable fees.

    Establishment and loan service fees are based on how much you borrow and the value of the property you use to secure the loan.
