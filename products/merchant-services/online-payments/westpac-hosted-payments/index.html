  • What you'll need to apply

    Am I eligible?

    • Your business needs to be registered in Australia
    • If you already have a Merchant ID number and you'd like to switch or add another Merchant facility, please call us on 1800 029 749 (24 hours a day, 7 days a week) or visit your nearest branch
    • Additionally, if you intend to accept online payments:
      • All prices must be in Australian dollars
      • You must settle your funds into a Westpac business transaction account
      • You will need to have a website domain name which matches your registered business name.

    What information do I need?

    • Your business details, including Australian Business Number (ABN), business structure, registered address and principal place of business
    • Financial information, including yearly turnover and average transaction value. If you provided these in your online quote, all we need is the quote number (valid for 14 days)
    • Depending on your business structure, details of every director, partner or trustee including date of birth, residence type and occupation.
