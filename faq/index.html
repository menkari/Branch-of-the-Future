Frequently Asked Questions

Have a technical mobile issue?

Report a problem by:

  • emailing us on online@westpac.com.au
  • calling us on 1300 655 505.

What is Westpac Mobile?

Westpac Mobile is a range of services, products, tools and calculators, customised for and accessible on your mobile.

Key features:

  • Mobile Banking
  • ATM, branch or banker locator
  • Online Investing
  • Tools and calculators.

What is Mobile Banking?

Mobile Banking allows you to access Westpac Online Banking and Business Online Banking from your mobile so you can:

  • View your Westpac account balances
  • View past transactions in each account
  • Transfer money between your Westpac accounts
  • Send money to your friends and family
  • Schedule and pay your bills
  • View your pending payments.

Business Online customers can also:

  • Switch between personal and business accounts
  • Pay staff
  • Schedule, manage and approve payments
  • Make faster payments using templates.

Which devices are supported?

Currently Mobile Banking is available on iPhone, iPod Touch, Android™ 2.2 (or above), and BlackBerry® 6.0 (or above) and Windows® 7.5 (or above). We’re working to support other operating systems in future.

Is Mobile Banking secure?

Westpac Mobile Banking provides the same high level security as our Online Banking. And with our Security Guarantee your money is protected if anything does go wrong, as long as you comply with our Online Banking Terms and Conditions. This includes keeping your access codes and passwords private.

Do I need to register for Mobile banking?

Not if you’re already registered for Online Banking or Business Online. If so, simply use your existing sign in details to access Mobile Banking. If not, call us on 132 032 (24 hours a day, 7 days a week), to register now.

You must also accepted the Online Banking Terms and Conditions.

I registered for Westpac Protect™ SMS code. Will I need to use it for Mobile Banking?

Yes, you’ll still need to enter the code we SMS to you every time you pay someone new or increase your daily limit.

I use a SecurID® token. Will I need to use it for Mobile Banking?

If you use Business Online, you may need to enter the security code generated by your token to authorise payments above your daily limit.

Where can I get help?

Call us on 1300 655 505 (8am-8pm, 7 days a week), for help with your Mobile Banking.

You can also click on the Help? icon at the bottom of all Mobile Banking screens for information about the page you’re on.

How much does Mobile Banking cost?

Mobile Banking is free but standard fees and charges for using Westpac Online and Business Online still apply.

You may incur internet data charges and call costs from your mobile service provider; as well as internet service provider costs when using a WiFi connection.
