  • Things you should know
    1. An Overseas ATM Cash Withdrawal Fee will not be charged for cash withdrawals made from ATMs belonging to institutions in the Global ATM Alliance. For cash withdrawals made from ATMs belonging to institutions outside of the Global ATM Alliance, an Overseas ATM Cash Withdrawal Fee applies to any successful cash withdrawal. Payable on the same day the transaction was made on the account or the next business day.

      Details of how to access your accounts using overseas ATMs and of participating institutions in the Global ATM Alliance are available in the 'A banking guide for overseas travel' brochure. Some overseas banks may also apply a surcharge to withdrawals from their ATMs. This should be drawn to your attention before you proceed with your transaction. This surcharge amount will be added to your withdrawal amount, converted to Australian dollars and debited to your account as one transaction.

    2. A Westpac Foreign Transaction fee is payable and will be incorporated into the $AUD transaction amount shown on your statement. The fee charged will be 3% of the $AUD transaction amount where a transaction is made in foreign currency and the merchant or financial institution accepting the card is located outside of Australia (comprising of 2% Westpac Processing Fee plus 1% Westpac On-Charged Scheme Fee).
